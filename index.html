About

Coco Homes offers a unique villa experience in the peaceful outskirts of Katra, near the Vaishno Devi shrine. A perfect blend of comfort and privacy, this luxury stay in Katra features premium amenities, including a sauna, steam bath, spa, bonfire, and private pool. Whether you're looking for an upscale hotel in Katra or an exclusive Airbnb, Coco Homes is the ideal choice for your next stay in Katra.

The hut features two beds, a spacious living room, and a bathroom equipped with steam and sauna baths. The open-area deck includes a private pool. Descend the stairs on the right side of the hut to find the in-ground firepit, Coco bar, and main kitchen. Enjoy a healthy meal under the open sky near the firepit. The upper lawn features a kitchen garden providing fresh in-house vegetables.
